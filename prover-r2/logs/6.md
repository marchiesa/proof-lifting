## Why This Is Hard

1. **Insertion sort verification**: Tracking the permutation property through insertion sort requires reasoning about how shifting elements right by one and placing the key preserves the multiset of values. 2. **Exchange argument**: The optimality proof requires comparing the greedy solution against all possible alternative solutions.
